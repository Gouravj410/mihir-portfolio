Introduction

I am an aspiring photojournalist based in Mumbai, focused on documenting news, street life, and real human stories. My work aims to capture honest moments as they happen, reflecting everyday realities and social events through a visual storytelling approach.

I am passionate about photography with a strong interest in news and documentary work. I regularly cover local events, street scenes, and human-interest stories, and I am keen to work as a freelance contributor with newspapers and digital media platforms.
